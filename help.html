Help & Support

Welcome to Nana DayCare's Help Center. Here, you'll find instructions and answers to common issues.

Managing Sitters

  • To register a new sitter, navigate to the Sitters page and fill in the required fields.
  • Sitter data includes name, location, gender, NIN, and contact information.
  • Ensure sitters are marked as "On Duty" to assign them to babies.

Managing Babies

  • Go to the Babies page to register or view babies.
  • Required information: name, age, gender, parent's name, and period of stay.
  • Record baby's time of arrival and departure accurately for proper tracking.

Payments

  • Babysitting fees are UGX 15,000 for half-day and UGX 20,000 for full-day.
  • Monthly payments can be processed via the Payments page.
  • Track payment history and generate reports for parents or administrators.

Procurement and Inventory

  • Manage daycare supplies like milk, fruits, diapers, and dolls via the Inventory section.
  • Track incoming and outgoing supplies to ensure stock availability.
  • Dolls can also be resold, and sales should be recorded for proper accountability.
